About

The Living Material Index is an experimental digital repository that supports ongoing research in Engineered Living Materials (ELMs). It provides a structure for documenting and sharing information across disciplines — from microbiology and materials science to design and architecture.

This site is collaboratively managed by the Engineered Living Materials Institute at Cornell University and the Microbial Assemblies Lab at Florida Atlantic University. It is intended as a research tool and reference archive, helping connect methods, tools, and outcomes that contribute to the emerging material culture of living systems.
