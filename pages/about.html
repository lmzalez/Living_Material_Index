About

The Living Material Index is an open, evolving resource that maps the emerging field of engineered living materials. It gathers methods, tools, and visual studies from across disciplines to make living-material research more transparent, accessible, and collaborative.

It is developed by the Microbial Assemblies Lab at Florida Atlantic University in collaboration with the Engineered Living Materials Institute at Cornell University.
