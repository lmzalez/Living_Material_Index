The Living Material Index is a working prototype for organizing knowledge in the field of Engineered Living Materials (ELMs). It serves as a shared digital repository that compiles protocols, tools, visual references, and related research.

Developed collaboratively between the Engineered Living Materials Institute at Cornell University and the Microbial Assemblies Lab at Florida Atlantic University, this site aims to provide a central reference point for researchers and designers exploring how living systems can be integrated into material practice.
